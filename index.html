How To Use

You can clearly see the two sections. First asking for starting and destination location and second asking for the classroom code.

Mode 1

  • Enter the starting and destination locations.
  • While filling the locations, you will see the dropdown list of locations you might be searching.
  • As soon as you fill both locations the map will show you the directions.
  • Once you reach the destination building, goto step 2.

Mode 2

  • The second section asks for the classroom code.
  • Press 'Enter' or Click the 'Lets Go' button.
  • Now you will be guided the way to your classroom by the popup messages on your screen.
  • Follow the instructions, and find your destination infront of you.
